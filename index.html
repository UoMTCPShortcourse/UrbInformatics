Brief Introduction about the Course

This professional course provides participants with advanced knowledge in urban informatics, geospatial technologies, and AI-driven solutions. It combines cutting-edge techniques such as field data collection, Web-GIS, remote sensing, AI-powered object detection, urban big data analytics, and drone applications. The program is designed for professionals seeking to transform spatial data into actionable insights for smarter planning, monitoring, and decision-making.

Day 1 – Warm-up Session

Content

  • Introduction to modern technologies (AI, Remote Sensing, IoT, Web-GIS, Drones).
  • Basics: GIS platforms, Google Earth Engine, Web-GIS interfaces.

Activities

  • Hands-on orientation with course software stack.
  • Case studies from Sri Lanka and globally.

Applications of Knowledge

  • Understand global technological trends.
  • Build readiness for advanced workflows.

Day 2 – Location-Based Services & Field Data Collection

Content

  • Mobile mapping using open-source apps.
  • Real-time database updating.
  • Dashboard visualization.

Activities

  • Field survey (socio-economic, tree inventory, utilities).
  • Sync to central DB + live dashboard.

Applications of Knowledge

  • Socio-economic surveys, tree inventories, utility surveys.
  • Agriculture plot monitoring.
  • Disaster rapid-damage assessments.

Day 3 – Web-GIS Apps & Multimedia Cartography

Content

  • Interactive maps with Leaflet/MapLibre.
  • Story maps, HTML tweaks, geotagging.
  • Sharing dashboards.

Activities

  • Publish Day-2 survey web map.
  • Add filters, charts, media popups.

Applications of Knowledge

  • Permit viewers, tourism story maps.
  • Disaster live maps.
  • Urban green atlases.
  • Crop watch portals.

Day 4 – Remote Sensing Analysis

Content

  • Google Earth Engine datasets.
  • Spectral Indices: NDVI, NDWI, NDBI.
  • Change detection & monitoring.

Activities

  • NDVI time-series crop analysis.
  • Flood/burn scar mapping.
  • Deforestation detection.

Applications of Knowledge

  • Crop monitoring, water mapping, urban growth.
  • Flood/disaster assessments.
  • Deforestation monitoring.

Day 5 – AI-Powered Object Detection

Content

  • Dataset preparation & labeling.
  • Train DL models.
  • Validation & GIS integration.

Activities

  • Train object detector (buildings, vehicles, crops).
  • Validate accuracy + publish map.

Applications of Knowledge

  • Building footprints, traffic vehicles.
  • Coconut/paddy detection.
  • Solar rooftops, coastal structures.

Day 6 – Urban Big Data & AI

Content

  • Urban data: social media, mobile, remote sensing.
  • Web scraping & preprocessing.
  • APIs & REST with GIS.

Activities

  • Sentiment dashboard with Maps API.
  • Scrape real estate datasets.
  • Connect web portals to GIS.

Applications of Knowledge

  • Public opinion analysis.
  • Land value monitoring.
  • Urban growth tracking.

Day 7 – Applications in Drone Tech

Content

  • Drone basics & regulations.
  • Live capture demos.
  • Industry expert knowledge.

Activities

  • Flight planning simulation.
  • Process orthomosaics/DSM.
  • QA/QC drone data.

Applications of Knowledge

  • Agriculture: crop monitoring.
  • Forestry canopy mapping.
  • Tourism 3D visualization.
  • Airport inspections.
  • Local authority mapping.
